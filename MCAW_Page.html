Mpumalanga Creative Africa Week (MCAW)

The annual Creative Africa Week takes place in November to celebrate the creative and collaborative work done by iBioscope teams in the Districts. It is also an annual awards ceremony that celebrates and encourages creative collaborations among various disciplines of creative industries in Film, Music, Fine Arts, Fashion, and Theatre.
